REFUND & CANCELLATION

The Orders placed by Buyers using the Platform are non-cancellable and non-refundable under the following conditions-

  • 1. If Buyer cancels his/her Order after One-minute of placing it, TOFOZ food Delivery service shall have a right to collect a penalty of 100% of the Order amount on Prepaid Orders.
  • 2. In the event if the address provided by Buyer is either wrong or falls outside the delivery zone.
  • 3. Failure to contact Buyer by phone or email at the time of delivering the Order booking
  • 4. Failure to deliver Buyer Order due to lack of information, direction or authorization from Buyer at the time of delivery.

Refunds

Buyer may be entitled to a refund for prepaid Orders if falls under these conditions.

  • 1. If the buyer cancels his/her Order within two-minute of placing Order.
  • 2. If the Order could not be delivered within 2 (Two) hours;
  • 3. If the Restaurant doesn't accept or cancels the Order due to reasons not attributable to Buyer, including but not limited to store being closed, non-availability of items, store cannot service online orders at that moment, store is overcrowded.

All refund amounts shall be credited to Buyer’s account as may be stipulated as per the payment mechanism of Buyer’s choice, the estimated time may take 2-7 working days as per Payment Gateway Company.

In case of the complaint of any spurious product, the liability shall solely lie with the Restaurant selling the product. TOFOZ food Delivery service is merely facilitating the transactions between the Restaurant and the Buyer and therefore, TOFOZ food Delivery service shall assume no liability with respect to the products sold by the Restaurant. TOFOZ food Delivery service strictly discourages dealing of any spurious product on its Platform and shall reserve the right to report such incidents to the concerned authorities for appropriate legal actions.
